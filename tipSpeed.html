Tip Speed: \[V_{ts} = \pi*d*rpm\] RPM: \[rpm = \frac{V_{ts}}{\pi*d}\]

What is the tip speed of an agitator with a 3" diameter rushton impeller spinning at 300 rpm? \[V_{ts} = \pi*d*rpm\] \[V_{ts} = 3.14*3\text{ inch}*\left(\frac{\text{m}}{39.37 \text{ inch}} \right)*300 \text{ rpm}* \left(\frac{\text{ min}}{60\text{ s}} \right)\] \[V_{ts} = 1.19 \text{m/s}\]
